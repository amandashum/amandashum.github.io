# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, technical leaders, collaborators, and project partners assessing Amanda's technical ability, product judgment, and working style.

## Product Purpose

Present Amanda's applied AI, computer vision, automation, and operational leadership work through clear evidence, interactive demonstrations, and accessible project narratives.

## Brand Personality

Precise, experimental, credible.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS dashboards, excessive gradients, playful cartoon styling, repetitive card templates, and decoration that obscures evidence.

## Design Principles

1. Show technical judgment through inspectable work.
2. Make complex systems understandable.
3. Use experimentation without sacrificing clarity.
4. Keep human review visible in AI workflows.
5. Treat motion as communication.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, adequate contrast, semantic structure, and reduced-motion preferences without removing meaningful feedback.
